About UPSC

This is a websitte for upsc asspirants.
It will contain various subjects included in upsc Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) is India's premier central recruiting agency responsible for appointments to and examinations for All India services and group A & group B of Central services.
The Civil Services Examination (CSE) is a prestigious competitive examination conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) in India. It is one of the most challenging and sought-after examinations in the country, attracting thousands of aspirants each year who aspire to join the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), Indian Police Service (IPS), Indian Foreign Service (IFS), and various other Group A and Group B central services.
Eligibility: Candidates must meet certain eligibility criteria, including age limit, educational qualifications, and nationality.
Syllabus: The syllabus for the examination covers a wide range of subjects, including Indian Polity, History, Geography, Economy, Science & Technology, Current Affairs, and Aptitude.

Selection Process

Candidates who qualify in the Preliminary Examination are eligible to appear for the Main Examination. Those who clear the Main Examination are then called for the Personality Test (Interview). The final selection is based on the cumulative marks obtained in all three stages.
